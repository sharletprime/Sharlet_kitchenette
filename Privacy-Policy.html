Sharlet Restaurant – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 9 November 2025

Sharlet Kitchenette ("the App", "we", "our", "us") helps users browse menus, view food posts, and manage restaurant operations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

When you use or register on the app (admin or subadmin), we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User role (admin / subadmin)

This information is used only for authentication and account management.

1.2 Location Information

Fine Location Coarse Location

Location is used to detect your country for availability settings and region‑based content. It is not stored or shared.

1.3 Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version

Used only for performance improvement and debugging.

1.4 Media Uploads

Admins or subadmins may upload:

  • Food images
  • Menu photos

Stored securely in Firebase Storage to display inside the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication and account login
  • Country detection to personalize experience
  • Creating and managing food posts
  • Updating stock, prices, and availability
  • Managing admin/subadmin access
  • Improving user experience
  • App security and performance

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

Google Firebase provides industry‑level encryption and protection. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not share your personal data with any third party except Firebase (Google) for cloud hosting.
  • We may share information with law enforcement only if legally required.
  • We do not share your data for advertising.

5. Permissions We Use

✅ INTERNET

To load posts, images, and sync data.

✅ ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION

To detect your country for app settings and location features.

✅ READ / WRITE Storage (if applicable)

To allow selecting and uploading food images. We do not access any data without permission.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Correct your information
  • Disable location permission
  • Sign out at any time

To request data deletion, contact us at: support@example.com

7. Children’s Privacy

Sharlet Restaurant is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be shown inside the app or on our website.
